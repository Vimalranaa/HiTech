Which Law Governs Fire Department NOC?

In India, the regulations that govern Fire Department NOC are covered under the purview of the 12th Schedule of the Indian Constitution, which comes under the ambit of the various municipalities. The fire-related activities are governed and administered by the State Governments under the Municipal By-Laws or State Regulations.

What are the Types of NOCs issued by the Fire Department?

Provisional Certificate

Once the verification of fire safety measures is done by the Fire Inspector. The Fire Department will then issue a Provisional NOC (No Objection Certificate).

Final No Objection Certificate (NOC):

Once the inspection is complete, the authority will again test and certify that the owner is abiding by all the recommendations and that all the installations are functioning in satisfactory condition.

Pre-Constructions Requirements

The application must provide information such as the business establishment's address, nature of the business, contact details, etc., duly signed by the concerned business owner.

Post-Construction Requirements

1. Upon receiving the declaration and compliance report by the owner, the technical officer will again visit the site. He will re-inspect the site and shall again forward his recommendation and opinion to the head of the Fire Department for providing a Final NOC.
